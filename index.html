About

Welcome to monoGames a metaverse that combines Play2Earn, Social2Earn (monoSol/monoMedia) and Sustainable2Earn (monoGreen) to create a unique, interactive experience which offers a range of interactive, earning opportunities for users through games, social interactions and sustainable actions. We hope by combining these different elements, we can create a unique and engaging experience for our community members to build and flourish in the metaverse.

Play2Earn

Play-2-Earn is a way in which players can earn rewards, such as in-game items like nft's and virtual currency, by simply participating in and engaging with the monoGames gaming metaverse. These rewards can be earned through regular gameplay, completing missions and tournaments.

Social2Earn

Social-2-Earn is a way in which players can earn rewards such as in-game items like nft's and virtual currency by promoting, interacting and engaging with the monoGames metaverse. Social players will help create, build and maintain a vibrant community.

Sustainable2Earn

Sustainable-2-Earn is a way in which players can earn rewards such as in-game items like nft's and virtual currency by participating in sustainable practices within monoGames and outside of monoGames. Sustainable players will help create and maintain a sustainable metaverse community.

Road Map

monoGames road map to success.

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Social2Earn Phase 1 Promote monoGames and earn some free monoGames tokens.

    All Gold & Silver Membership holders can now start receiving their daily air drops of monoGames tokens.

  • 3/9/2023

    Social2Earn Phase 2 monoMedia

    Social2Earn Phase 2 monoMedia to connect our community together to share our metaverse experiences within monoGames.

  • 6/12/2023

    NFT's For monoGames

    NFT's for monoGames will be ready to be distributed and for sale.

  • 6/12/2023

    Play2Earn Phase 1 monoGames Destiny Of The Universe Missions

    Play2Earn Phase 1 monoGames Destiny Of The Universe Missions will begin and will entail the story line of monoGames and the goal of every Faction to win the Star Trophy and rule monoGames.

  • 3/3/2024

    Social2Earn Phase 3 monoSol

    Social2Earn Phase 3 monoSol is the virtual world of monoGames where all players will have interactive avatars that connect to the monoGames metaverse.

  • 3/9/2024

    Play2Earn Phase 2 monoGames Destiny Of The Universe Game

    Play2Earn Phase 2 monoGames Destiny Of The Universe Game will begin and the goal of every Faction is to win the Star Trophy and rule monoGames.

  • 9/12/2024

    Sustainable2Earn

    Adding sustainability to the monoGames metaverse to help build the future of sustainability between the metaverse and the real world, rewarding players for their sustainable actions.

  • 3/3/2025

    Faction Elections Begin

    This is where every player in his or her Faction gets to vote for their leadership in their blockchain community.

  • 9/3/2028

    Expansion Begins

    Here is where the monoGames community gets to vote on 3 new blockchains to be part of the monoGames metaverse.
